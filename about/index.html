The organization

Learning by doing.

DO Question Make Test Share

Through student-driven experiential learning, STEM Fellowship builds skills in data analytics, research, and scholarly communication. Its programs connect modern STEM knowledge with practical questions in schools and communities.

The organization brings these opportunities to students through a growing national network of high school chapters, university branches, regional communities, mentors, and partners.

Mission: To create equitable learning opportunities, connections, and experiences for students to develop modern skills for the digital age.

What guides the work

Five core pillars.

Together, these pillars connect national learning opportunities with meaningful action in local communities.

01

Data Science Education for Change

Students develop skills in data analytics, problem-solving, critical thinking, and cybersecurity to address issues in their communities.

02

STEMpowerment for Diversity and Equity

Mentorship and research programs help students from underrepresented backgrounds pursue opportunities in STEM across Canada.

03

Science Communication for All

Young researchers gain the skills to present their work clearly and communicate scientific knowledge to others.

04

Grassroots for Local Communities

High school chapters, university branches, and regional networks bring national programs into local communities.

05

Fellowship Development

Partnerships, outreach, and shared networks expand STEM Fellowship’s projects, connections, and reach.
