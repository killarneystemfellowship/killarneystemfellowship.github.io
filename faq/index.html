01Are the workshops really free?→

Yes. Our current classroom workshops are offered at no cost and include the planned activity materials and trained student presenters.

02Which grades are the workshops designed for?→

Engineering bridges is designed for Grades 4–7, while Strawberry DNA extraction is best suited to Grades 5–7. Share your class grade in the request form and we’ll help confirm the fit.

03How long does a visit take?→

Each workshop is designed for one class block. We coordinate the exact timing with the teacher when confirming the visit.

04What does the chapter bring?→

We bring the planned workshop materials and trained presenters. The classroom teacher provides the learning space, normal classroom supervision, and any school-specific guidance we should know.

05Where are visits available?→

Our outreach is intended for Vancouver-area elementary classrooms. Submit your school through the request form and we’ll confirm whether the chapter can coordinate a visit.

06What happens after I submit a request?→

Our team reviews your school, grade, class size, workshop preference, and possible dates. We then follow up by email to discuss availability and the next steps.

Still have a question?

Send the chapter your classroom details and we’ll help determine the right next step.
