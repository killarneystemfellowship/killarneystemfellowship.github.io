Five ways into STEM

What our members actually do.

You do not need to arrive with a finished project. Start with the discipline that catches your attention and build from there.

01

Outreach

Turn science into something students can touch. Members train as presenters and bring free, materials-included workshops to Vancouver elementary classrooms.

02

Data & AI

Ask useful questions of real-world data, learn the foundations of analysis, and prepare for national opportunities such as the High School Big Data Challenge.

03

Publication

Move from an idea to a clear scientific argument through literature review, scholarly writing, peer feedback, and science communication.

04

Physics

Explore open-ended problems through experimental design, theory, teamwork, and the structured debate used in young physicists’ tournaments.

05

Research

Build the habits behind good research: precise questions, careful methods, honest evidence, and the confidence to learn from mentors.
